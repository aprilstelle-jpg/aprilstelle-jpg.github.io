About Me

Born and raised in Morristown, NJ, I have always emjoyed writing and storytelling. I began writing for my school's football team in my junior year of high school, and I have been passionate about writing ever since. I am currently a sophomore at Syracuse University, majoring in Magazine, News and Digital Journalism. I am excited to pursue a career in journalism after graduation, and I am eager to gain experience in the field through internships and other opportunities. In my free time, I enjoy reading, watching documentaries, listening to music, and going to concerts.

I am a staff writer for The Good Life magazine here at Syracuse University, where I write for the Lifestyle section. I have also writtern for the Culture Section of the Daily Orange newspaper where I have written about events on campus and feature stories covering the culture of Syracuse University and the surrounding area. I am also a member of Zeta Phi Eta fraternity, the professional communications fraternity, where I have held the posiiton of Historian chair for two semesters. These opportunities have allowed me to gain experience in the field of journalism and have helped me to develop my writing skills. I am excited to continue to grow as a writer and journalist.

I am on the Magazine track at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications, and I am particularly interested in writing for magazines and other long-form journalism. I am also interested in digital journalism and the ways in which technology is changing the field of journalism. I am excited to explore these interests further through my coursework and internships, and I am eager to see where my career in journalism takes me after graduation.
